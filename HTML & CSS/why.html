Why is this important?

“ Your future depends on your dreams, so go to sleep.” – Mesut Barazany.

As known by everyone around the world, sleep is the main factor in everyday working efficiency. Unfortunately, many people lack good judgment in when they should go to bed in order to wake up fully refreshed and energized. If sleep is optimized, it will be beneficial for all hard-working people in the world. More work will be accomplished, people will feel much better, and health and wellbeing will be increased. As a program, this can be very advantageous to people from all aspects of life.
